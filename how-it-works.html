How Our Quran Classes Work

A simple and structured process to help your child begin their Quran learning journey with confidence.

01
📅

Step 1 – Free Trial Class

Parents book a free trial class where the teacher meets the student and understands their current level.

02
🔍

Step 2 – Student Assessment

During the trial class, the teacher evaluates the student’s reading ability, pronunciation, and familiarity with Arabic letters.

03
✍️

Step 3 – Personalized Learning Plan

Based on the assessment, a personalized learning path is designed according to the student’s level and pace.

04
🎓

Step 4 – Regular Classes Begin

Students start structured one-to-one classes following the Noorani Qaida Personalized Program and gradually progress toward Quran reading.

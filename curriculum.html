Our Educational Curriculum

A detailed breakdown of our structured Quranic learning phases.

Noorani Qaida Personalized Program

A structured one-to-one Quran foundation journey for children and beginners.

Format

One-to-One Online Classes

Duration

3–5 Months

Focus

Makharij, Harakaat, Shaddah & Madd

Outcome

Transition to Quran Reading

This program is designed to help children and beginners build strong Arabic reading foundations step by step. Students progress from letter recognition and pronunciation to confident Quran reading through a structured four-phase journey.

Structured Personalized Progressive Quran-Focused

Learning Outcomes

By the end of the Noorani Qaida Personalized Program, students will achieve the following learning outcomes.

01

Recognize all Arabic letters individually

02

Pronounce each letter correctly with proper Makharij

03

Apply Harakaat (Fatha, Kasra, Damma) while reading

04

Understand Tanween, Sukoon, Shaddah, and basic Madd rules

05

Read Arabic words and short Quranic phrases with confidence

06

Transition smoothly from Noorani Qaida to Quran reading

Phase 1 – Arabic Letters & Makharij

Phase 1 focuses on introducing students to the Arabic alphabet and establishing correct pronunciation of each letter.

  • Objective: Build a strong foundation in recognizing and articulating Arabic letters with proper Makharij.

Learning Outcomes

  • Recognize all Arabic letters individually
  • Pronounce each letter correctly with proper Makharij
  • Distinguish between similar sounding letters
  • Identify letters in different visual forms

Teaching Method

Slow and clear pronunciation, demonstration of mouth position, and interactive drills.

Final Assessment

Oral assessment to identify letters randomly and pronounce them correctly without hesitation.

Phase 2 – Harakaat & Word Formation

Phase 2 introduces students to fundamental vowel signs and how to combine letters to form simple words.

  • Objective: Understand how vowels change letter sounds and learn to blend letters into simple syllables and words.

Learning Outcomes

  • Read letters with Fatha, Kasra, and Damma
  • Recognize and apply Tanween and Sukoon
  • Combine letters to form 2-3 letter words
  • Read short syllables with confidence

Teaching Method

Demonstrating sound changes, repetition drills, and slow blending practice.

Duration: 3-4 weeks

Final Assessment

Assess ability to read Harakaat, Tanween, and Sukoon in simple 2-3 letter words accurately.

Phase 3 – Shaddah & Basic Madd Rules

Phase 3 introduces students to more advanced reading elements used in Quranic recitation. Students will learn how Shaddah affects pronunciation and how basic Madd rules extend vowel sounds.

  • Objective: Master advanced reading elements like Shaddah and Madd rules to read longer words with correct rhythm.

Learning Outcomes

  • Correctly read letters with Shaddah
  • Recognize and apply basic Madd rules
  • Understand how Shaddah changes pronunciation
  • Read longer Arabic word combinations
  • Maintain correct rhythm during reading

Lesson Progression

Teaching Method

Focusing on Shaddah effect, pause/stretch for Madd, and guided reading of longer words.

Duration: 3-4 weeks

Final Assessment

Assess pronunciation of Shaddah, application of Madd, and reading of longer words confidently.

Phase 4 – Transition to Quran Reading

Phase 4 prepares students to transition from Noorani Qaida to actual Quran reading. Students begin reading short Quranic words and simple verses while applying all learned skills.

  • Objective: Develop fluency and confidence while transitioning from Qaida to reading actual Quranic text.

Learning Outcomes

  • Read simple Quranic words confidently
  • Apply all rules while reading
  • Maintain correct Makharij during recitation
  • Read short Quranic verses smoothly
  • Ready to begin regular Quran reading (Nazra)

Final Assessment
